# Rules of Inference
## De Morgan's laws
[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/De_Morgan%27s_laws#Extension_to_predicate_and_modal_logic)

$$\forall x\,P(x)\equiv \neg [\exists x\,\neg P(x)]$$
$$\exists x\,P(x)\equiv \neg [\forall x\,\neg P(x)]$$
